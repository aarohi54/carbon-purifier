About the Model

The Carbon Purifier is an innovative model created to address the issue of air pollution caused by factory emissions. Using a smart mechanism involving a conveyor belt with carbon-absorbent cloth, our model captures carbon particles before they are released into the atmosphere. This cloth rotates and is cleaned using water, making it reusable and eco-friendly. Our aim is to promote sustainable, cost-effective, and scalable solutions to environmental challenges.

How It Works

Our model functions in the following steps:

  • Factory emits air containing carbon particles.
  • A cloth-based conveyor belt is placed at the exit point of the chimney.
  • The cloth traps carbon particles as the air passes through.
  • The conveyor rotates the cloth through a water tank.
  • In the tank, the carbon is washed off, making the cloth clean and reusable.
  • The collected carbon can be disposed of or potentially reused in other processes.

Why This Model is Important

  • Reduces carbon emissions from factories.
  • Encourages reuse of materials and sustainable practices.
  • Low cost and low maintenance design.
  • Uses common materials, making it accessible for real-world application.
  • Helps educate people on pollution control and climate action.

Future Scope

Our current model is designed on a small scale to demonstrate the concept. However, it has the potential to be implemented on a larger industrial scale with real-time carbon capturing and processing systems. Future upgrades could include:

  • Automation and sensor-based carbon detection.
  • Use of advanced filtering materials.
  • Data tracking for emission levels.
  • Integration with factory control systems for auto-cleaning cycles.
